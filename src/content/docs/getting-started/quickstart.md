Getting started with ruumy takes less than a minute.

<Steps>

1. **Open ruumy**

   Go to [ruumy.xyz](https://ruumy.xyz) in your browser.

2. **Create a room**

   Click **New Room**. A unique URL is generated instantly — no account needed.

   ```
   https://ruumy.xyz/abc123
   ```

3. **Share the link**

   Copy the URL and send it to anyone — via message, email, or just paste it anywhere.

4. **People join**

   When someone clicks the link, they enter the room immediately. You'll see them appear in the presence sidebar.

5. **Start collaborating**

   Chat in real time. Move your cursor. Add notes to the canvas.

</Steps>

## That's it

No onboarding flow. No settings to configure. No permissions to manage.

The room is live as long as at least one person is in it.

:::tip
Rooms are ephemeral by default. If you want to save the content, copy it before leaving. Persistent rooms with history are coming in a future update.
:::
